About

Baldrick's Towpath Tipple is a unique floating canal bar serving drinks to boaters, towpath walkers, and canal lovers across the UK waterways.

Operating as a roving canal trader, the bar travels the canal network bringing independent beer, wine and good company to events, floating markets and towpaths.

Our mission is simple: celebrate canal culture, support independent breweries, and create a welcoming waterside meeting place wherever we moor.

The Boats

Baldrick

Baldrick is the historic motor boat that powers the operation, towing the bar wherever the canals take us.

Tipple

Tipple is our converted butty boat bar (formerly TAM), currently being transformed into a dedicated floating pub with solar power, improved electrics and a proper beer dispense system.

Together, Baldrick and Tipple create a travelling canal bar like no other.
