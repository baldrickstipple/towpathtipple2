About

Baldrick’s Towpath Tipple is a unique floating canal bar travelling the UK waterways. Serving real ale, cider, wine and bar snacks, we bring a friendly canalside atmosphere to festivals, towpaths and boating gatherings.

The project centres around two historic narrowboats — the motor boat Baldrick and the butty boat Tipple. Together they form a travelling canal pub, celebrating waterways heritage and great independent drinks.

The Boats

Baldrick

Baldrick is the motor boat powering the operation. She tows Tipple across the canal network, bringing the bar wherever the waterways lead.

Tipple

Tipple (formerly the butty TAM) is being converted into a dedicated floating bar with solar power, upgraded electrics and proper beer dispense.
